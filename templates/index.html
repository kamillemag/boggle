Let's Play Boggle!

How to Play: create words given random assortment of letters displayed on the board. Words can be created from adjactent letters; letters which are horizontal or vertical of each other as well as diagonals. The letters must connect to each other in the proper sequence to spell the word correctly. This means that the next letter in the word can be above, below, left, or right of the previous letter in the word (excluding any letters previously used to construct the word).
